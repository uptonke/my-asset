# v9.6 Trade Ticket Explainability

v9.6 explains why a manual trade ticket exists or why no ticket was generated. It translates gate failures, missing manual inputs, missing cash, incomplete real-world prices, and missing order lines into plain-language review notes.

Boundary:
- Explanations are for governance and review only.
- They are not buy/sell advice.
- They cannot enable execution or broker submission.
